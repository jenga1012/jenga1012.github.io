About Swiftlink Logistics

Connecting Businesses, Delivering Excellence

Our Story

Swiftlink Logistics (Pvt) Ltd is a Sri Lanka-based freight forwarding company specializing in comprehensive end-to-end logistics solutions.

We leverage our extensive global network of partners, including major shipping lines and airlines, to offer seamless connectivity and competitive rates. Our strategic location in Sri Lanka positions us as a key hub for trade in the region and beyond. We are committed to simplifying international trade for our clients through expertise, technology, and a customer-centric approach.

Our Vision

To be the leading freight forwarding and logistics partner in Sri Lanka, recognized globally for our reliability, innovation, and commitment to customer success in international trade.

Our Mission

To deliver superior logistics solutions that drive efficiency and growth for our clients by:

  • Providing reliable and timely transportation services across air, ocean, and land.
  • Simplifying complex customs procedures with expert brokerage.
  • Leveraging technology for transparency and real-time tracking.
  • Building strong, collaborative relationships with clients and partners.
  • Offering customized solutions tailored to specific industry needs.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to exceeding expectations through technology and a customer-first philosophy.

Technology Driven

We invest in cutting-edge logistics technology to provide real-time tracking, data analytics, and streamlined communication, ensuring full visibility and control over your shipments.

Customer Focused

Your success is our priority. We work closely with you to understand your unique challenges and develop tailored logistics strategies that meet your specific requirements and goals.
